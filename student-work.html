Student Work

Photoshop Composition

This design was in my first design class. The goal was to combine 50 images into a single image to portray a song. The song that the design portrays is 'Fix You' by Coldplay. This project was meant to learn how to use the tools in Photoshop to blend and mask images together.

Branding

We were given an animal to create a logo for and I was given a rabbit. Through research, I found that aside from its attributes of long ears, quickness, and hopping, in folklore, a rabbit uses its cunning to outwhit its enemies. So I created a logo to mimic that idea.
